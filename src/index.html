Coronavirus positive test rates as high as 35% in parts of King County
Of the 55,000 King County residents tested for coronavirus from November 28-December 12, more than 9,800 (17.8%) were positive for the virus. In South Auburn, the positive-test rate hit 34.8%. The biggest increases since mid-November were in Bothell/Woodinville, Southeast Kent and Issaquah, all up nearly eight percentage points.
